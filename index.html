ABOUT AAROH

AAROH is a non-governmental organization (NGO) in Nepal, officially registered under the Society Act with the District Administration Office in Kathmandu (459/070/071). It is affiliated with SWC, the Social Welfare Council (affiliation number: 38685), licensed under the Nepal Government Act. It was established on January 29, 2014.

AAROH is dedicated towards taking positive actions to transform the lives of underprivileged children and women through education. We offer support and facilitation of various projects that enhances and bring growth in a community.
